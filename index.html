The story behind our barber shop

Welcome to Gentlemen's Barber Club, the embodiment of class in the vibrant city of Rotterdam. We pride ourselves on understanding our customers' needs, ensuring an enjoyable experience at our shop.

Specializing in both modern and classic haircuts, we offer a curated selection of timeless styles that have stood the test of time. From flattops and pompadours to boogies, contours, and other iconic old-school signature cuts, we embrace the artistry of the past.

Join us as we invite you to take a seat in one of our chairs and indulge in the finest service. Together, let's shape the future of grooming excellence.
